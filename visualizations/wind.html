Latitude vs. Max Wind Speed

The question for this part of the project was to understand whether or not Latitude has any effect on wind speed.

By looking at the plots of the data, there may be some minimal effect of latitude on max wind speed.

Wind speed between -20 and +20 degrees tended to be lower than locations at higher/lower latitudes.

The difficulty in truly determining this, as the southern hemisphere has far less land mass at higher latitudes.

In general, this may be an effect of the Coriolis force which will drive higher wind speeds farther from the equator.
